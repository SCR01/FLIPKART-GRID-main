GRID 6.0 - Smart Vision Technology Quality Control

A leading solution leveraging smart vision technology for advanced quality control, powered by OCR and IR.

Smart Vision Technology

Smart vision technology uses advanced imaging systems and algorithms to capture and analyze visual information. In the context of quantity and quality testing, it helps automate the quality inspection process by identifying a product, its quantity, and any defects or quality attributes.

Features

OCR for Brand Details

Automatically detect and extract brand names from product packaging using advanced OCR technology.

OCR for Expiry Date

Quickly identify and retrieve the expiry date from product labels to ensure product freshness and safety.

IR-based Counting

Utilize infrared (IR) sensors for accurate counting of items, improving inventory management and automation.

Freshness Detection

Detect the freshness of products using advanced sensor technology to ensure optimal quality and reduce waste.
